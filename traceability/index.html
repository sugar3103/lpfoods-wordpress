Commitment.

We are committed to offering superior quality seafood products without compromising the environment, the welfare of the fish, the welfare of everyone working on it. We scout the world in search of sustainable seafood, and outstanding people to join our community. A recognised seafood leader and a country fascinating rich in traditions, Vietnam is where our heart is.

Our seafood comes from these selected partners.

Nha Trang Seafoods

  • Meet the first seafood processing factory established in the beautiful costal city of Nha Trang, Vietnam.
  • A responsible company, it is actively investing in farms to have full control of everything it produces, and guarantee perfectly safe and high quality products to consumers
  • It is certified ASC, Global Gaap, Best Aquaculture Practices for his efforts to minimize environmental impact. It is also certified SMETA, ISC 9000, Sedex, for his attention to the wellness of all its workers whose livehood depend on the company.

Promarmex
